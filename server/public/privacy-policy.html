Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

Privacy Summary

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Account tokens and sync data are necessary for the service.
  • AtlasAnalytics loads only after analytics consent.
  • If analytics is enabled, successful login events and limited account context may be linked to your current AtlasAnalytics session.
  • When you are signed in, your cookie preference ID and optional choices may be stored against your account as a consent audit record.

1. Who We Are

StreamBooru sync services are operated by Amateur-God with analytics infrastructure provided by Atlas Tech Solutions Ltd. For UK GDPR purposes, Atlas Tech Solutions Ltd is the controller for AtlasAnalytics personal data processed through optional analytics technologies.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: username, avatar, Discord identifier when linked, password hash for local accounts, and encrypted site credentials you choose to sync.
  • Sync data: favourite post keys and configured booru site settings.
  • Consent audit data: when signed in, your browser consent ID, policy version, optional choices, consent timestamps, and optional HMAC hashes of request metadata for audit integrity.
  • Technical data: request metadata needed for security, rate limiting, and service operation.
  • Analytics data: where you allow analytics, AtlasAnalytics may collect page views, interaction events, platform context (web, desktop, Android), login flow events, and client-side error diagnostics.

3. AtlasAnalytics

AtlasAnalytics is optional. It only loads after you allow analytics in the cookie banner. If you allow analytics and sign in, we may link that login to your current AtlasAnalytics session using your username/account identifier and limited login context. We do not send passwords, tokens, full IP addresses, or full user agents to AtlasAnalytics as event properties.

4. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to processing, portability, and withdraw consent. Contact admin@atlastechsolutions.co.uk for data protection enquiries.
